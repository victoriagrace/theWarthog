Warthog Instructions

Download code from Github and run it in Xcode to open up theWarthog. 

Usage: 
- Click the snout to turn synth on and off
- The horizontal slider under the snout controls the gain and cutoff of a low pass filter
- The slider to the left of the snout controls the frequency of the synth
- The slider to the right of te snout controls the formants of the synth (the top making an "oo" sound and the bottom making an "ee" sound)
